My Professional Journey

My cover letter

Hello, I'm Igor, 27 years old, and I've always aspired to work with technology. In 2015, I began my academic journey in the Electrical Engineering course at the Federal University of Paraíba before shifting towards Systems for the Internet. Throughout the course, I engaged in significant projects, mostly focused on education.

In 2016, I served as a programming instructor in a public school, sharing knowledge of logic in C and programming in Arduino with the students. Following this experience, I became a mentor in the "PetCare" project, focused on developing an application to control the feeding of small and medium-sized animals. Although I don't have the GitHub code link, we were featured in an article illustrating our initiative.

Alongside my Electrical Engineering studies, during the 2020 pandemic, I initiated studies in mathematics, nurturing my passion for teaching. During this period, I took on the roles of a school monitor and substitute teacher.

In 2022, I made the decision to pause my Electrical Engineering course to fully focus on software development, an area I have been exploring since the pandemic. Despite already possessing knowledge in programming, logic, and automation from electrical engineering, I chose to transition to a more specialized course. Even with nearly 70% of the course completed, I believed this change was essential to pursue my true passion.

I am excited about this new phase and confident that my diverse experience will bring a unique perspective to the field of software development. I am open to opportunities and eager to contribute my knowledge and dedication to innovative projects.
